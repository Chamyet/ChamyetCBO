Chamyet Community based organization is an idea that was born in the year 2008 out of the need to keep vulnarable children from Mujini informal settlements in school. Mujini slums in Kericho County (KENYA), is a home to over 300 households and whith each household estimated at 4.4 souls.

About

The Journey Started with an idea of an ECD school to give needy Children a chance to access basic education and together with like minded individuals, the school was started at KCC area next to the Mosque. Over the years the school has been supporting the needy children from Mujini slum and its environs through three pillars namely Education, Nutritional support and Psychosocial support

Mr Henry Sanya

Chairperson, Chamyet CBO

We have been depending on donations from the founding members and well wishers to support programs aimed at supporting vulnarable children also considered as needy.

Ms Mwanaidi Bidui

Treasurer, Chamyet CBO

The organization is Currently Supporting 30 households and 90 vulnarable children within Kericho County, with the intentions of reaching out more children in future subject to availability of resources.
